Connecting Your Business with the Swedish Market

Marketing your business in a foreign market can feel daunting – and reasonably so. You've put a lot of thought into how your brand is presented and invested time and money into ensuring your copy is on point. Naturally, you want to safeguard that carefully crafted image when you enter a new market.

I have been a localization specialist for the Swedish market for eight years, with 2.3 million source words of adapted content under my belt. If there is one conclusion I can draw from my experience, it is this: when it comes to marketing content, standard translation isn't good enough.

When you put a marketing message out into the world, you are not primarily communicating information; you are communicating a tone and a vision, and inviting people to buy into that vision.

Successfully transferring your marketing message to a foreign market is a delicate and complex process, that requires:

  • A deep understanding of your vision and a keen awareness of subtle cultural nuances in both the source and target cultures,
  • The creative copywriting skills to adapt and remold your content to resonate with your foreign target audience and achieve your marketing goals, and
  • The ability to navigate this process while staying true to your brand.

This is not a standard translation task. For this, you need a transcreator. And the good news is you’ve found one.

I’m Andreas, and I help interesting businesses connect with interested customers in the Swedish market.

My Process

We Connect

Our journey begins with a call where we get to know each other. During this conversation, you'll share what you need help with specifically, and I’ll aim to understand your needs, what drives you, your product or service, your motivations, and your marketing goals. We'll also establish the scope of the project and a timeline. After our discussion, you'll receive a quote from me.

I Do My Homework

You send me the content you need adapted and any reference material you have. The more detailed the information, the better. I’ll be happy to review previous marketing materials, your tone of voice, the history of your company — anything that can help me immerse myself in your brand.

I Get to Work

I dive into the content you need transcreated. This process can be as interactive as you want. Depending on the scope of the project, I am happy to provide you with updates and partial deliveries, so you can verify I am on the right path.

First Delivery

Even if you don’t speak Swedish, don’t worry! I’ll help you understand my thought process, by providing a rationale for every part of the Swedish copy, explaining the decisions behind each element.

Rounds of Feedback and Revisions

Your input is invaluable. If the first delivery meets your expectations, great! If you have any thoughts or feedback, I invite you to share them with me so we can ensure you feel completely satisfied with the final product.

Final Delivery

Once you are completely satisfied with the content, I will deliver the final version to you. And with your new, culturally adapted content, you'll be ready to make an impact in the Swedish market, presenting your brand as if it were a native entity.
